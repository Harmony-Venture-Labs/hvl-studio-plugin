# Operations Mapping

A research prompt that maps the end-to-end operations of a business
in a given industry and revenue band — operational steps,
departments, pain points, technology gaps, financial transaction
points.

**Used automatically by:** `generate-thesis` during its
desk-research step. Claude reads this file, runs the research, and
returns the data to the calling skill. Nothing for the user to do.

**For standalone use:** copy the prompt below, paste it into
ChatGPT or Claude.ai, and replace `[INDUSTRY]` with the industry
and `[REVENUE]` with the revenue band (for example `$5M` for a
mid-market operator, `$50M` for upper-middle).

---

Map the complete end-to-end operations for a [INDUSTRY] business doing $[REVENUE] annually. Include:
1. 10-15 key operational steps
2. Departments involved
3. Current pain points
4. Technology gaps
5. Financial transaction points

Format as visual workflow.
