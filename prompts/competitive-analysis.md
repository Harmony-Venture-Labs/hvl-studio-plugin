# Competitive Analysis

A research prompt that scans the software vendor landscape in an
industry — existing solutions, solution age, price ranges, common
complaints, and market gaps.

**Used automatically by:** `generate-thesis` during its
desk-research step. Claude reads this file, runs the research, and
returns the data to the calling skill. Nothing for the user to do.

**For standalone use:** copy the prompt below, paste it into
ChatGPT or Claude.ai, and replace `[INDUSTRY]` with the industry
you're scanning.

---

Research the software ecosystem for [INDUSTRY]. Provide:
1. List of existing software solutions by category
2. Age of major solutions (mark any 10+ years old)
3. Price ranges for typical solutions
4. Major complaints/limitations mentioned in reviews
5. Market gaps where no good solution exists

Focus on businesses with $1M-$50M revenue.
